Asteroids Game

Controls:
"w" to accelerate
"A" to rotate left
"D" to rotate right
"S" to decelerate
click anywhere to shoot
space bar to hyperspace
"E" to restart
"Help! How do you play?" Hover here!
Theres a lot so bear with me:
shoot the asteroids for points
don't get hit by the asteroid or you will get hurt
collect the blue orbs to add time (ship go there, don't try to shoot it)
collect medkits to regenerate health
once in a while you will see a purple present float by
this will regenerate health and add time
